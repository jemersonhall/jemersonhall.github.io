Lastly, A Brief Snipet Of My Resume and Story

Summary

Born and raised in Scottsdale, AZ. I went to Harvard University for undergrad where I studied Pscyhology. After Harvard, I spent the next four years at a boutique strategy consulting firm in Boston called Alliance Consulting Group. I had a fantastic experience and was able to gain valuable experience with clients ranging from Fortune 500 staples (i.e., Coca-Cola and Harley-Davidson) to private equity firms making acquisitions of small to mid sized firms.

After finishing up my work with Alliance, I decided to get my MBA and began B School at the Kellogg School of Management in August 2014. At Kellogg, I am focusing on Finance, Strategy, and Entreneurship classes (like this coding class!) and am involved in a variety of clubs such as Kellog Board Fellows, Insight Group mentorship, and Ski and Snowboard club.

Post Kellogg, I will be getting married on July 9, 2016 in Spring Lake, Michigan and then moving out to San Francisco to began my post-MBA career at Google. I will be working in Strategy for the Android and Chrome for Work team and cannot wait for this new chapter of my life to begin.

Alliance is a boutique, strategy consulting firm with clients ranging from small privately-held companies to large Fortune 500 corporations that work across a variety of industries.
